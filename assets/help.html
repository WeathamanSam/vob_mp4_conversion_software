1. Folder Setup

VobReel scans for folders named exactly VIDEO_TS.

Required Structure:

/My_DVDs
  /Wedding_Tape
      /VIDEO_TS   <-- (VobReel looks here)
  /Vacation_2004
      /VIDEO_TS

Tip: Select the top-level "My_DVDs" folder as your source.

2. Quality Settings

  • Best Quality (CRF 18)
    Essential for VHS transfers. It preserves the analog "grain" ensuring the picture doesn't look plastic or blurry.
  • Standard (CRF 20)
    The industry standard balance. Best for digital camcorder footage.
  • Compact (CRF 23)
    High compression. Use this only if you need to email the files.
